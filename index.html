Sameer Rao began his musical journey at the age of eleven. Initially he was trained by Pandit Veerabhadraiah Hiremath in Mysore. In 2002 Sameer Joined Vrindaban Gurukul in Mumbai, where he was groomed by Padma Vibhushan Pandit Hari Prasad Chaurasia Ji himself, in the traditional Guru-Shishya Paramapara. An "A" Graded Artist and a recepient of awards such as 'Surmani', 'Nadakishore' 'Yuvapratibha' etc., Sameer has performed extensively in India and Abroad.
